## Strategic Gaps: Why & Priority

Not "you need 8 investors." Instead:

### Investors (2 / 8 target) — Priority ★★★★★

**Current:** 2 identified  
**Target:** 8  
**Why:** 
> Investors bring three things: (1) Credibility — room perceived as serious, business-focused; (2) Introductions — to other founders, scaling companies, funding rounds; (3) Funding conversations — sponsors get ROI perspective. **Impact:**
