PART 2 — AUTONOMOUS DAILY OPERATION
Every morning at 7am the system runs the following sequence automatically for every Active campaign:
Step 1 — Prospect Discovery
Run the search engine against the campaign parameters. Find new prospects matching the target audience, job titles, sectors, and locations. Web Search and Companies House run based on campaign settings.
Step 2 — Contact Verification
For every new prospect found — immediately run RocketReach to find the named decision maker. Full name, job title, verified email, LinkedIn profile URL. Any prospect where RocketReach cannot find a named contact is quarantined — not added to the live pipeline — and flagged for manual review.
Step 3 — Deduplication
Before saving any prospect — check if the company or individual already exists in the pipeline for this campaign. If duplicate — skip. Never add the same prospect twice.
Step 4 — Email Drafting
For every new verified prospect — draft a personalised outreach email using the campaign tone, campaign description, and contact details. Use the Claude API. The email is specific to the prospect — not a generic template.
Step 5 — Approval Queue
Every drafted email goes to the Approval Queue tagged with the campaign name. If the campaign approval rule is Manual — the email waits for human approval. If the campaign approval rule is Auto — the email is sent automatically at 9am without human review.
Step 6 — Sending
Approved or auto-approved emails send at 9am. Never send outside 9am to 5pm Monday to Friday. Never send on weekends or UK public holidays.
Step 7 — Reply Monitoring
Every 15 minutes the system polls for replies. Every reply is classified automatically — Positive, Not Now, Unsubscribe, Out of Office, Auto Reply, Unclassified.
Step 8 — Pipeline Movement
Based on reply classification the system automatically moves the prospect through the pipeline:

Positive reply — move to Meeting — send immediate notification to info@eventperfekt.com
Not Now reply — move to Contacted — schedule a follow up in 30 days
Unsubscribe — move to Lost — add email to suppression list immediately
Bounce — move to Lost — flag for manual review
Out of Office — no pipeline change — reschedule follow up for when they return if date is in the email

Step 9 — Daily Summary
Every day at 6pm send a summary email to info@eventperfekt.com showing for each active campaign: prospects found today, emails drafted, emails sent, replies received, positive leads, and any items requiring attention.
