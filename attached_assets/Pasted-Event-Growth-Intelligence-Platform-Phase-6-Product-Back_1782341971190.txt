Event Growth Intelligence Platform – Phase 6 Product Backlog

Epic 1 – Market Intelligence
Priority: Critical

* Add Insights as the first page.
* Automatically research market demand before strategy.
* Show competitor events.
* Show pricing benchmarks.
* Show sponsor activity.
* Show audience trends.
* Produce a Market Opportunity Report.

Epic 2 – Personas & Discovery
Priority: Critical

* Generate audience personas automatically.
* Generate sponsor personas.
* Generate media personas.
* Build Apollo search filters automatically from approved personas.
* Add exclusion rules (e.g. coaches, influencers, community builders).

Epic 3 – Messaging Studio
Priority: High

* Generate:
    * LinkedIn connection requests
    * First LinkedIn message
    * Follow-up sequences
    * Email outreach
    * Sponsor outreach
    * VIP invitations
    * Speaker invitations
    * Media outreach
* Human approval required before sending.
* Learn from reply rates and improve recommendations.

Epic 4 – Presentation Studio
Priority: High

* Integrate Gamma API (or equivalent).
* Generate:
    * Sponsor decks
    * Event brochures
    * Speaker packs
    * Partnership proposals
    * VIP invitation packs
* Allow generated presentations to be attached directly to outreach.

Epic 5 – Relationship Intelligence
Priority: High

* Explain why each prospect is recommended.
* Highlight relevant company and role signals.
* Surface engagement insights where available.
* Improve prospect prioritisation with AI reasoning.

Epic 6 – Elizabeth
Priority: High

Transform Elizabeth into a proactive Growth Coach.

Examples:

* Ticket sales are slowing.
* Sponsor outreach is underperforming.
* LinkedIn is outperforming Instagram.
* Suggested next actions.
* Daily growth recommendations.

Epic 7 – Learning Engine
Priority: Future

After every campaign, automatically learn:

* Best-performing messages.
* Highest-converting channels.
* Best-performing audiences.
* Best sponsor sectors.
* Best campaign timings.

Apply these learnings to future events automatically.
