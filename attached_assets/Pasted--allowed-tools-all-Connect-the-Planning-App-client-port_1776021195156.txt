Connect the Planning App client portal to the Group Staff Portal for ALLI Foundation.

PART 1 — MEETINGS AND EVENTS VISIBLE IN CLIENT PORTAL

When EP staff create a meeting or event linked to ALLI Foundation in the Planning App:
- It appears in the client portal under a new Calendar tab
- Client sees: meeting title, date, time, description, who is attending from EP
- Client can Accept or Decline
- Their response is logged and EP team gets a notification

Add to client portal navigation:
Calendar tab — shows upcoming meetings and events
Each item: title, date, time, EP attendees, Accept button green, Decline button red
Accepted shows green tick
Declined shows grey — EP team notified

When client accepts: email to info@eventperfekt.com — [client name] has accepted [meeting name] on [date]
When client declines: email to info@eventperfekt.com — [client name] has declined [meeting name] — please reschedule

PART 2 — RAID SUMMARY IN CLIENT PORTAL

Add a Project Status tab in the client portal showing:
A simplified risk summary — not the full RAID detail — just what the client needs to know

Show three sections:
Active Risks: any open risks at Medium or above — show title and mitigation summary only — no severity scores
Open Issues: any open issues affecting the client — show title and what EP is doing about it
Resolved: recently closed items — shows client EP is on top of things

Do NOT show: financial risks, internal EP risks, or anything marked internal only
Do NOT show severity scores or probability ratings to the client
Keep language positive and professional — risks described as items being monitored

PART 3 — AUDIT TRAIL IN PLANNING APP

Mirror the audit trail from the Group Staff Portal in the Planning App staff view.

In /epglobal/clients — ALLI Foundation detail page:
Add Activity tab showing all audit log entries for this client in chronological order.
Most recent first.
Each entry: timestamp, action type badge, description, performed by.

PART 4 — INVOICE VISIBILITY IN CLIENT PORTAL

Add Payments tab in client portal:

Shows all invoices for this client:
Invoice reference, description, amount, date issued, status badge
Pending — amber
Paid — green
Overdue — red

Download invoice PDF button per invoice.

When invoice is sent by EP team client gets email notification:
Subject: Invoice from Event Perfekt Global Ltd — [reference]
Body: A new invoice is available in your client portal. Log in to view and download.
