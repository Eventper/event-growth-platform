The email generation architecture is fundamentally incorrect.

The platform is currently producing generic emails with a different title.

For example:

"I Am Her — PR Pitch: Women in Leadership"

This is wrong.

There should never be a generic email template called "PR Pitch."

Instead, every email should be generated from the communication objective.

The system should first determine:

* Who is receiving this?
* What publication or organisation is it?
* What do they normally publish?
* What type of story are they asking for?
* What outcome do we want?

Only then should it generate the email.

Examples:

Journalist asking for a human-interest story:
Subject should be something like:

* Why I Left London After Building My Career There
* The Hidden Cost of Success for Women
* The Women Everyone Thinks Are Fine
* Why More Professional Women Are Quietly Rethinking Success

NOT:
"I Am Her PR Pitch"

Beauty journalist:
Subject:

* Why Skin of Colour Needs Different Summer Skincare Advice
* The Beauty Devices Esther Actually Recommends
* The Home Treatments That Are Worth Your Money

NOT:
"I Am Her Beauty Pitch"

Corporate sponsor:
Subject:

* Exploring a Strategic Partnership
* Supporting Women Who Lead
* Partnership Opportunity

NOT:
"I Am Her Sponsor Pitch"

Podcast:
Subject:

* Story Idea for Your Audience
* A Conversation About the Hidden Cost of Success

The campaign name should almost never appear in the subject line unless it is already a recognised brand.

The campaign should appear naturally within the body where relevant.

Also remove generic labels such as:

* PR Pitch
* Outreach Email
* Sponsor Email
* Guest Email

These are internal categories, not user-facing content.

The AI should produce emails that look as though they were written manually by an experienced communications professional.

Every message should be unique.

The platform must never generate two emails that only differ by replacing a few words.

Finally, add a "reasoning layer" that determines the best communication strategy before writing:

1. Communication objective
2. Recipient type
3. Story angle
4. Subject line
5. Opening hook
6. Supporting narrative
7. CTA

Only after those steps should the AI generate the final email.

The user should receive communications, not templates.
