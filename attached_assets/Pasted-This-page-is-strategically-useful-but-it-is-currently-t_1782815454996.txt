This page is **strategically useful**, but it is currently **too broad**.

It is trying to sell:

**platform + event + corporate wellbeing + brand partnerships + international expansion + waiting list**

Too much.

For now, this page should do one job:

**Make The Woman Who Leads The Room feel like the first serious expression of a bigger platform.**

Not sell everything at once.

## What is good

The platform name works:

**The Human Behind The Title**

That is strong. It gives depth behind the dinner.

The core idea is also strong:

> Leaders are not just titles. They carry pressure, identity, health, confidence, money, wellbeing and responsibility.

That is the platform.

## What feels weak

### 1. “Leadership wellbeing platform” is overused

You say it too many times. It starts to sound like corporate wellbeing language.

Use it once. Then speak plainly.

### 2. “Community” appears again

You know the rule. Avoid “community” for now.

Replace:

**community opportunities**

with:

**future experiences, partnerships and speaker opportunities**

### 3. “Professionals” is too broad

In the flagship experience section, replace:

**professionals**

with:

**senior professionals**

Better:

**female founders, executives, directors, senior professionals and business owners**

### 4. “Personal development” in brand partnerships weakens it

Remove **personal development**.

It attracts coaches and trainers. Wrong signal.

### 5. Corporate section is useful but too soon

Keep it, but don’t overbuild it yet. You have not sold the first room. Corporate programmes can come later.

For now, make it softer:

**Corporate Conversations & Leadership Wellbeing**

not full “Leadership Development” yet.
