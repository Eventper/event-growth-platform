2. Why Milton Keynes?

Title:
The unexpected city break.

Body:
Milton Keynes is perfectly positioned between London, Birmingham and the Midlands, making it easy for guests travelling from across the UK.

London Euston is approximately 30 minutes by train, Birmingham is easily accessible by rail and road, and the city sits close to major routes including the M1. For women travelling from London, Birmingham, Manchester, Scotland or further afield, Milton Keynes offers a convenient and stylish central meeting point.

It is also one of the UK’s fastest-growing cities, home to major employers, innovation-led businesses, professional communities, green spaces, lakes, shopping, restaurants and family-friendly neighbourhoods.

Title:
