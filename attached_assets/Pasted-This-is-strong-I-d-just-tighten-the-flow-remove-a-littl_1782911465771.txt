**Why This Evening Exists**

The most accomplished women rarely get a room of true peers — women from other industries who understand the weight of leadership without needing it explained.

This evening exists to create that room.

A room for women who have built, led, decided, carried responsibility and stayed standing through different seasons of personal challenge, business growth and leadership at a high level.

The mission is to reshape how women are recognised, supported and retained in leadership. Not only by celebrating success, but by making visible the realities that shape longevity: confidence, visibility, women’s health, financial health, emotional load, identity, decision-making and the pressure of carrying responsibility at a high level.

The vision is bigger than one dinner. It is to build a platform that helps organisations, employers, partners and cities understand what women need in order to keep leading, keep building and keep growing through every stage of life and business.

This first room is where that conversation begins.

Because the right room changes everything.
